About Me

A little bit about who I am and where I come from

Hi, I am Neliswa

I am a graduate software developer from Durban, KwaZulu-Natal. I am naturally quiet but I make up for it in curiosity and passion for learning new things. I am always digging into how things work and finding ways to build them better.

Some of the work I am most proud of includes a WIL Management System I built at the MUT Innovation Lab, which handled everything from student placements to mentor approvals and report generation. I also built a Job Tracker to help manage and organise job applications in one place.

I have been working with HTML, CSS and JavaScript since my second year at university, and the CodeTribe Web Development Bootcamp gave me the opportunity to go back to those foundations with fresh eyes and sharpen them further. I enjoy working across the full stack and I am always looking to grow whether that is picking up a new framework, writing cleaner code, or understanding a concept at a deeper level than before.
